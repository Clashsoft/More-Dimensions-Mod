{== Potions ==}

- Potion of Insanity
  The Insanity effect will allow you to interact, gather, and break blocks that are unbreakable when sane.
- Flash Freeze Potion
- Anti Poison
- Water Walking Potion
- Potion of Sanity
- Potion of Order
  The failed version of the Insanity potion would be a Potion of Order. Drinking the potion in the Paradise of Chaos would kill you and warp you back to the standard vanilla dimension. Death Message:"Prof.  Oak says "You can't use that here!""

- PoC Fire Resistance potion
  Causes the player to spontaneously combust during the duration.  
- PoC Water Walking potion
  Gives you water breathing and water walking.
