{== Magic ==}

>> New systems of magic, one of which is herbalism , plant, and regen based.

--- Mana ---

- Each player has it's own mana
- Maximum mana is 20
- Mana can be refilled with Mana Stars
- Maximum Mana can be increased with Mana Stones

--- Spells ---

- PoC -> random things
- Nether -> fiery things
- End -> spells fail to work

--- Staves ---

- Staff of the Zero
  A rare Staff that uses zero Mana.
- Explosive Staff
  A Staff that creates an explosion where it is used
